# Task 24: SLA, Service Lifecycle & Subscription Management (Easy)

## Simple Definition

Service Level Agreement (SLA) defines availability commitments. Subscription management controls access, billing, and governance in Azure.

## Objective

To understand SLA guarantees, service lifecycle stages, and subscription management in Azure.

## Architecture Overview

Azure Subscription  
↓  
Services  
↓  
SLA & Lifecycle Management

## Steps

### Step 1: Review SLA Information

1. Go to Azure Portal.
2. Search Service Level Agreements.
3. Review SLA for:
   - Virtual Machines
   - Storage
   - SQL Database.

### Step 2: Manage Subscription

1. Go to Subscriptions.
2. View subscription details.
3. Check payment method and billing cycle.
4. Assign roles under Access Control (IAM).

### Step 3: Review Service Lifecycle

1. Visit Azure Updates.
2. Review Preview, General Availability, and Deprecated services.

## Verification

* SLA documentation reviewed.
* Subscription details accessible.
* Role assignments visible.
* Lifecycle stages understood.

## Conclusion

Successfully explored SLA, subscription management, and service lifecycle concepts in Azure, demonstrating governance and cloud service planning knowledge.
